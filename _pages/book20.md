Evolution Biology All Logic
# Evolution Biology All Logic

![Profile Picture](evolution.jpeg)

○ 이 책은 영어로 제작되었습니다.

I started collecting biological knowledge for the first time in 2009. I began gathering interesting biological knowledge that I acquired while reading books because I didn’t want to lose it. As I continued, I encountered similar information from different sources. Some information was incorrect, while some was the same. There were also cases where one piece of information was a subset of another. The more I organized the pile of knowledge, the more I encountered these situations.

This biology series consists of six volumes in total. The fifth volume bridges evolutionary theory and the sociobiological complexity that arises from individual interactions, presenting a broader view of collective biological behavior.
